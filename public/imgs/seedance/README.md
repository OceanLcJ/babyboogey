# Seedance 2.0 图片替换指南

请生成以下图片，并保存到当前目录 (`public/imgs/seedance/`)，文件名需完全匹配。

## 1. Hero Banner (首页大图)
*   **文件名**: `hero.png` (或 .jpg, .webp)
*   **推荐比例**: 16:9 (例如 1920x1080)
*   **AI 提示词 (Prompt)**:
    > A cute, modern, horizontal hero image for a baby dance video website. Features a happy baby dancing with subtle AI/technology glowing effects in the background. Bright, colorful, high quality, photorealistic but slightly stylized. 16:9 aspect ratio.
    > (中文释义：一张可爱的、现代的宽屏大图，用于宝宝跳舞视频网站。画面中有一个开心的宝宝在跳舞，背景带有微妙的 AI/科技感光效。色彩明亮，高质量，写实但带有一点风格化。)

## 2. 介绍图 (Introduce)
*   **文件名**: `introduce.png`
*   **推荐比例**: 3:2 (例如 1200x800)
*   **AI 提示词 (Prompt)**:
    > A conceptual illustration of AI technology processing a baby photo. Shows a photo transforming into a digital wireframe or glowing silhouette, representing the Seedance 2.0 AI process. Clean, modern, tech-focused style.
    > (中文释义：一张展示 AI 处理宝宝照片的概念图。显示一张照片正在转化为数字线框或发光剪影，代表 Seedance 2.0 的 AI 处理过程。干净、现代、科技感风格。)

## 3. 功能特性图 (Items)
推荐比例 4:3 (例如 800x600)

*   **文件名**: `cloud.png` (云端/免下载)
    > **Prompt**: A 3D isometric illustration of a cloud server connecting to a mobile phone, representing cloud computing and no download needed. Soft blue and white colors.
*   **文件名**: `quality.png` (高清画质)
    > **Prompt**: A split screen comparison showing a blurry video on the left and a crystal clear 4K video on the right, labeled with a '2K' badge. High tech style.
*   **文件名**: `easy.png` (操作简单)
    > **Prompt**: A happy parent holding a smartphone with a very simple interface, pressing a single big button. Minimalist and friendly.
*   **文件名**: `speed.png` (极速生成)
    > **Prompt**: An illustration of a lightning bolt combined with a video play button, representing fast video generation speed. Dynamic and energetic.

## 4. 深度解析图 (Deep Dive)
推荐比例 4:3 (例如 800x600)

*   **文件名**: `consistency.png` (肢体一致性)
    > **Prompt**: A digital human skeleton overlay on a dancing baby, showing precise joint tracking and stability. Tech medical style but cute.
*   **文件名**: `physics.png` (物理模拟)
    > **Prompt**: Close up of fabric texture flowing naturally in the wind, representing realistic physics simulation in 3D rendering.
*   **文件名**: `expression.png` (生动表情)
    > **Prompt**: A grid of 4 cute baby faces showing different happy expressions (laughing, smiling, surprised, winking), high detail 3D render.
*   **文件名**: `fast.png` (极速)
    > **Prompt**: A stopwatch or timer with a rocket taking off, symbolizing fast processing time.

---
**操作步骤**:
1. 使用 Midjourney / Dall-E / Stable Diffusion 生成上述图片。
2. 将图片重命名为对应的文件名 (如 `hero.png`)。
3. 放入 `public/imgs/seedance/` 文件夹中。
4. 页面会自动更新显示。
