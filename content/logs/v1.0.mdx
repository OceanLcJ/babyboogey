Welcome to the first public beta of **BabyBoogey** — an AI baby dance generator that animates a single photo into a short, shareable dance video.

## What’s included

- Generate a dancing video from a single photo
- Choose from multiple dance templates (with preview + duration)
- Smoother defaults for prompts (including a built-in negative prompt)
- Download your result as MP4

## Tips for better results

- Use a clear, well-lit, front-facing photo
- For close-up portraits, try gentler / lower-motion templates
