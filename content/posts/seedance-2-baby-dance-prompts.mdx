## Seedance Baby Dance Prompt Formula

Use this structure:

```text
[subject] + [dance style] + [setting] + [expression] + [motion quality] + [safety/style notes]
```

Example:

```text
Adorable baby doing a gentle boogie dance, bright smile, cozy nursery background, soft lighting, smooth realistic motion, family-friendly, short vertical video.
```

Good prompts usually include:

- Subject: baby, toddler, infant, little kid.
- Dance style: boogie, hip-hop, disco, salsa, waltz, cute wiggle.
- Mood: happy, playful, sweet, funny, wholesome.
- Setting: nursery, party room, living room, holiday scene.
- Quality: smooth motion, natural face, stable body, HD.
- Format: short clip, vertical video, social-ready.

## Prompt Settings That Usually Help

If your tool lets you control format or duration, start simple:

- Duration: 5 to 8 seconds for first tests.
- Format: 9:16 for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.
- Motion: low or medium for close-up portraits.
- Quality: HD if available.

## Common Seedance Baby Dance Mistakes

### Prompt is too busy

If you ask for a dance, costume, complex camera move, background change, special effects, and multiple characters at once, the output can become unstable. Start with one subject and one clear dance style.

### Motion is too intense for the photo

Close-up baby portraits do not usually handle fast full-body dance prompts well. Use gentle motion for close-ups and stronger dance prompts only when the torso or full body is visible.

### The prompt ignores privacy

Do not include personal names, addresses, school names, or private family details in prompts. Keep the prompt focused on style and motion.

### The prompt fights the image

If the photo has a sleeping baby, a prompt asking for extreme breakdancing may look unnatural. Match the energy of the prompt to the source photo.
